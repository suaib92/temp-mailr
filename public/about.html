About TempMailr

Protecting your privacy since 2020

Our Mission

TempMailr is built to safeguard your identity in a digital world. We provide secure, disposable email addresses that help you avoid spam, bypass verification traps, and stay anonymous during short-term interactions online.

Features

  • No registration required
  • Automatic email deletion within 1 hour
  • 24/7 global service availability
  • Mobile-friendly user interface
  • Fast and secure email inbox

Why Use TempMailr?

We know how frustrating it is to receive spam after signing up for a one-time offer or service. TempMailr gives you control over your inbox without the need to share your personal email. Whether you’re testing apps, exploring newsletters, or registering temporarily, TempMailr ensures peace of mind.

Our Technology

TempMailr is powered by scalable cloud infrastructure with advanced spam filtering and real-time mailbox creation. We are constantly improving our system to enhance speed, reliability, and user privacy.

Our Team

Founded by privacy-focused developers, TempMailr is a small team dedicated to building open, secure, and ethical tools that respect digital freedom. We believe that temporary inboxes are a basic right—not a premium service.
