Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

TempMailr does not require any personal registration or identity verification. We do not store user identities, IP addresses, or usage logs. All interactions with the platform remain anonymous.

2. Data Retention

All emails received by a temporary address are stored for a maximum of 24 hours. After that, they are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers. No backup or recovery is possible.

3. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for site performance and session continuity. These cookies do not track your activity outside our service or collect personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

TempMailr may use third-party providers like Google AdSense to serve relevant ads. These services may use anonymized cookies to display non-intrusive advertising. No personal user data is shared with advertisers.

5. Children's Privacy

TempMailr is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from anyone under this age.

6. Your Control

You may close your temporary email session at any time. Since we do not associate emails with any account, there is no need to opt-out manually. Data expires automatically.

7. Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use after any update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may reach out via our Contact Page or email us at privacy@tempmailr.in.
