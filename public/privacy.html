Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

At TempMail, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, store, and protect your data when you use our temporary email service.

1. Data Collection
We do not collect personally identifiable information. The temporary emails generated are not tied to your identity.

2. Usage Data
We may collect basic usage metrics (like number of emails generated, device type, etc.) to improve our services. These are always anonymous and used internally.

3. Cookies
Our website may use cookies for analytics and session functionality. You can choose to disable cookies via your browser settings.

4. Data Retention
Emails and associated data are stored only temporarily and deleted after a predefined time window.

5. Third Parties
We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third-party advertisers or marketers.

6. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at support@tempmail.example.
