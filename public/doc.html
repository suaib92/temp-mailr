❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long do emails stay in my inbox?

A: All messages are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

Q: Can I recover deleted emails?

A: No, deleted emails are permanently removed from our servers.

Q: Is this service really free?

A: Yes! TempMail is completely free to use, supported by non-intrusive ads.
