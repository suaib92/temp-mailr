❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long do emails stay in my inbox?

A: 24 hours from the time they are received. Then they are deleted permanently.

Q: Can I recover deleted emails?

A: No. Once an email is deleted, it is erased securely with no backups.

Q: Is this service really free?

A: Absolutely. TempMailr is 100% free to use, made possible through minimal, non-intrusive ads.
